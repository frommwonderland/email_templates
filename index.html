Who We Are

Outputfield is an organization amplifying the power of local artist communities into a global space. We provide an international mutual care network for experimental artists, curators, venues, and galleries. Our mission is to neutralize the canon and offset hegemony in the arts.

What We're Doing

Currently we are building the website, which will feature an internal directory for artists to connect, and conspire, and a showcasing platform to exhibit their collaborations.
