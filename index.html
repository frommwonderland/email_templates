Who We Are

Outputfield is an organization amplifying
the power of local artist communities into a
global space. Our mission is to provide
mutual care, opportunities and resources to
benchmarking experimental artists.

What We're Doing

We war currently working on launching our full
website, which will feature the private online
directory for artists to find collaborations, support,
and audience, and a project archive to show the
things they make together.
