Skin Garden: Reconsider Flesh
opens with a stream of theory

For our 3rd and final opening, we present Reconsider Flesh: a breathing sculpture garden nestled inside walls sculpted by Salomé Chatriot. Come inside to see dozens bodies sculpted by 3D artists around the world, while hearing Bella Baguena's soundscape. Every corner of Reconsider Flesh is an audiovisual embodiment of sonic and bodily range.

Come tune in live via zoom on 6/18, 2PM EST (8PM CET), and hear thoughts on the multiplicity of bodies, affects of desire, and other inspirations behind Reconsider Flesh.
