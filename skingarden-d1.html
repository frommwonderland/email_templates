Today is the first day of Skin Garden’s 3-day opening. Today, we are opening up the Lobby in New Art City and celebrating on Zoom with 4 DJ sets, hosted by bien agiter.

Skin Garden: Lobby opens
with housewarming party

The Skin Garden lobby will be our living room for the next 3 days. Starting today, you can take a stroll inside and hear Tooba Greer Williams’ soundscape travel through Kushagra’s fleshy architectures. Once open, all 3 rooms will be accessible through this lobby, including Walk-ins Welcome (which opened in March).

To celebrate our first day, bien agiter and SOAK are hosting 4 DJ sets over Zoom starting at 2PM PST. Tune in for some experimental sounds from Online Threat, ASH-ANTI, softmatter, and Venus in Foil.
